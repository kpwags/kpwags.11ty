## Cooking

### Tilapia with Salsa Verde

I've made this a few times before. I think I saw Alton Brown in an episode of [Good Eats](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0344651/) use Salsa Verde as a topping for tilapia and you know what, I think it pairs well. I just season tilapia fillets with salt and pepper and bake them for 10-12 minutes at 375°F. Dress each fillet 1-2 tablespoons of Salsa Verde and serve. Goes well with some potatoes and a vegetable like zucchini or asparagus.
