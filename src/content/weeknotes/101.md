## Becky Aikman Book Event

On Thursday my wife and I ventured to Wayne for dinner and then a book event at the Radnor library. Becky Aikman was there for a talk about her book [Spitfires: The American Women Who Flew in the Face of Danger During World War II](https://bookshop.org/p/books/spitfires-the-american-women-who-flew-in-the-face-of-danger-during-world-war-ii-becky-aikman/8f6139cab9395ee4). It was an interesting talk and an interesting topic and true story that I didn't know about. Hearing Becky talk about how she pieced together the story from diaries and ancestors was fascinating and I'm looking forward to reading the book.
