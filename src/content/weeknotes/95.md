## It's Always Something

This week in the adventures of home ownership is our oven. I turned it on to pre-heat for roasting a pork tenderloin and the element sparked. I'm not sure if it was a one-time thing or not, but now we have to monitor it closely if and when we use it. We are getting a new element to replace it, but there's always something going on needing replacement.

## Too Hot in April

It touched 90ºF (32ºC) here this week. I'm sorry, that's July and August weather, not mid-April. I do not like it and I'm glad it's cooled off some. I want spring-time weather, not summer just yet.

## Started Work on My Photography Site

I set it as a goal this year and I've started laying out the pages. It's still early on, but I'm liking this initial look and feel for it.

## Lots of Hockey

The I-League is coming up on its final week, but the Bombers started their season. We played Sunday and Thursday nights, winning 7-1 & 6-4 respectively. Starting out the season 2-0. This I-League season hasn't been our best, we're 3-5-1 and will be playing for pride. I'm looking forward to more hockey.
