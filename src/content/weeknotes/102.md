## Free Weekend

For the first time in a little while, we have a weekend with nothing going on. No plans, nothing...it's great. I spent Saturday writing, working on my data repository, and lifting. Today will probably be more of the same. It's nice to just sometimes veg with nothing to do.

## Widow's Bay

## Cooking

### Homestyle Chicken Curry

I found this [recipe](https://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/food-network-kitchen/homestyle-chicken-curry-9590573) in a Food Network magazine and I decided to be a little adventurous. The aroma during and after cooking was great, but the dish itself felt like it was missing something. I've also never had curry, so I don't really have anything to compare against. It was also definitely a weekend meal as it took a little more time than I would prefer weeknight meals to take. One could probably speed up cooking by microwaving the potatoes for 7-10 minutes prior to putting them in.
