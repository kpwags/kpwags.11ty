## Project Hail Mary

My wife and I saw [Project Hail Mary Tuesday](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt12042730/) on IMAX and it was really good. Having recently re-read the book in anticipation of the movie, I was impressed with how well they kept true to the spirit and the content of the book. I'd *highly* recommend going to see it.

## Progress on the Sunroom

I put the first coat of paint on the windowsill and trim on one side of the sunroom. It looks really nice. It might be white paint against primer, but the white pops. Which is what I want. I'm looking forward to getting the paint for the bead board to bring in some color.

## Pokémon FireRed

Succumbing to nostalgia, I picked up Pokémon FireRed for the Nintendo Switch. So far it seems to hold the magic the original Pokémon Red & Blue had back in the day.
