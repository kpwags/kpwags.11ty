I still can't believe it's already April. The year has been flying by. I'm not sure how to feel about that part, but I am glad the weather is beginning to turn nicer.
<!-- excerpt -->

## Roller Coaster Weather

The weather in March was kind of crazy. It hit 80º at one point only to drop below freezing the next day. The weather would get warm, cold, hot, cold...it was kind of funny not knowing how to dress to take Tess for her morning walk.

## Moving Back to More Analog

This month I started switching back to notebooks and pens and pencils. There really is something about physically writing something down. I've been journaling more and experimenting more with using pen and paper to keep track of life.

## Sick

Mid-month I caught a nasty cold. Knocked me out for a weekend and more. It does make me glad to be able to work from home. I was feeling well enough to work once the worst of the weekend went by, but didn't have to burn sick days to keep co-workers from getting sick.

## Running
<li></li>
I started the month out well and even got some distance runs in. I'm not sure if it was whatever bug I picked up limited me, but my end-of-month runs didn't go as well. Oh well, still got some runs in which was nice since the weather was cooperating.
